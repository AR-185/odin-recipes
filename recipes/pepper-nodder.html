Pepper Nodder

Home

Description

These Scandinavian ginger cookies are a holiday staple and will make a welcome addition to your cookbook! This recipe makes many small cookies and uses mostly simple ingredients you most likely already have in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Ground Cardamom
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Ground Ginger
  • Ground Black Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350
  2. Mix butter and sugar till smooth, then beat in eggs one at a time.
  3. Add flour and spices to mixture.
  4. Form mixture into small ~1 inch cookies
  5. Bake for 10 mins.
  6. Transfer to wire racks and let sit till cool.
