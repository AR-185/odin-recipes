Description

A classic Italian-American dish, this pizza recipe will make both family dinners and friendly get-togethers easy and enjoyable.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 6 oz Tomato Paste
  • 1 tsp Ground Oregano
  • 1 tsp Ground Basil
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Crust

  • 3 1/4 Cups All-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 oz Yeast
  • 1 tbs Sugar
  • 1 1/3 Cups 120F Water
  • 1/3 Cup Oil

Toppings

  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheese
  • 6 oz Meat of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425, Grease two pizza pans.
  2. Whisk together sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Combine 2 cups of flour with yeast, sugar, and salt.
  4. Add the warm water and oil, slowly add remaining flour.
  5. On floured surface, knead dough then split in two.
  6. For each pizza: sauce then add cheese followed by meat, bake till golden brown.
