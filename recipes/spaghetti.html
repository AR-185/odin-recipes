Pepper Nodder

Home

Description

This is spaghetti, you know what it is.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion, Chopped
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 16 oz Tomato Sauce
  • 6 oz Tomato Paste
  • 2 tsp Oregano, Dried
  • 2 tsp Basil, Dried
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper, Ground

Steps

  1. Mix beef, onion, garlic in a saucepan over medium-high heat till browned, 5-7 minutes.
  2. Drain grease.
  3. Add the remaining ingredients.
  4. Simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
